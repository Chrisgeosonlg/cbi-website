How to Apply

Application Process

Applying to CBI is straightforward. Follow these steps to begin your theological training journey.

  1. Complete the online application form
    Fill in your personal details, programme choice, and study mode on this page.
  2. Attach your academic certificates
    Upload copies of your Form IV or equivalent certificates. Minimum entry requirement: CSEE (Form IV pass).
  3. Submit a personal testimony
    A 1–2 page testimony of your faith journey and call to ministry, with a reference letter from your pastor.
  4. Pay the application fee
    TZS 10,000 via M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, or Airtel Money. Paybill number: 123456.
  5. Await confirmation
    Our Admissions Office will contact you within 7 working days via WhatsApp or email.
