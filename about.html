Our Story

God's Faithfulness
Since 1988

Founded in 1988 by Calvary Assemblies of God, Calvary Bible Institute (CBI) was established with a singular vision: to raise up a generation of Spirit-filled, biblically grounded ministers for the Church in Tanzania and across East Africa.

From our first graduating class of twelve students, we have grown to serve over 1,200 alumni now serving as pastors, evangelists, missionaries, and church leaders across more than 15 countries.

CBI is governed by the leadership of Calvary Assemblies of God and operates with full commitment to Pentecostal doctrine, biblical integrity, and excellence in theological education. We are proud to offer both residential campus programmes in Dodoma and fully online courses accessible throughout East Africa.

✝️

Our Foundation

We believe the local church is central to God's plan, and that well-trained ministers are essential to its health and growth in Africa.

Theology

Statement of Faith

CBI upholds the foundational doctrines of the Assemblies of God movement, rooted in Scripture and consistent with classical Pentecostal theology.

📖
The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God — the supreme rule for faith and conduct.

✝️
The Triune God

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

🕊️
Baptism of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues, as in Acts 2, available to all believers.

🌅
The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the imminent, personal, and visible return of Jesus Christ our Lord.
