Governance

Institutional Leadership

CBI is governed by experienced, Spirit-filled leaders committed to academic excellence and biblical integrity. Our administration upholds the values of Calvary Assemblies of God and ensures CBI fulfils its mission to train ministers across East Africa.

CH
Rev. Dr. Emmanuel Mwanga
Chancellor
Overall spiritual oversight, vision & institutional governance of CBI

Rev. Dr. Mwanga has served as Chancellor since 2010. He holds a Doctor of Theology from Nairobi International School of Theology and has over 25 years of ministry experience.

✉ chancellor@calvarybi.ac.tz
PR
Rev. Dr. Grace Mkumbwa
College Principal
Academic leadership, faculty development & institutional quality

Rev. Dr. Mkumbwa holds a PhD in Biblical Studies and has led CBI's academic expansion, including the launch of the online learning platform.

✉ principal@calvarybi.ac.tz
VP
Rev. Stephen Kileo
Vice Principal
Student affairs, campus life & co-curricular activities

Rev. Kileo oversees all student welfare matters, campus discipline, and spiritual formation activities at CBI.

DS
Rev. Dr. Esther Nyamasyo
Dean of Studies
Curriculum design, examinations & academic standards

Rev. Dr. Nyamasyo holds a Doctor of Ministry and is responsible for all curriculum development and examination coordination at CBI.

AD
Mr. Thomas Wambura
Academic Director
Programme development, accreditation & quality assurance

Mr. Wambura manages CBI's programme development and works with external accreditation bodies to maintain quality standards.

RG
Mr. Abel Shirima
Registrar
Student records, admissions, transcripts & certification

Mr. Shirima manages all student records, processes applications, and coordinates the issuance of certificates and transcripts.

✉ admissions@calvarybi.ac.tz
FO
Mrs. Ruth Kasanga
Finance & Admin Officer
Budget management, fees, bursary & financial reporting

Mrs. Kasanga manages all financial operations including student fees, bursary awards, and institutional budget planning.

✉ finance@calvarybi.ac.tz
IT
Mr. Joseph Mushi
ICT Director
Website, online learning platform & digital infrastructure

Mr. Mushi oversees all technology operations including the website, student portal, online courses, and digital payment systems.

LB
Mrs. Amina Suleiman
Librarian
Physical & digital library, research support & resources

Mrs. Suleiman manages CBI's growing library of theological resources, including the new online digital library of downloadable materials.

CP
Rev. Paul Makundi
College Chaplain
Spiritual formation, chapel ministry & student welfare

Rev. Makundi leads the spiritual life of the college, including daily chapel, prayer groups, and pastoral support for all students.

SA
Miss Judith Komba
Student Affairs Officer
Student welfare, counselling, alumni relations

Miss Komba provides pastoral and practical support to students and maintains relationships with CBI's growing alumni network.

MC
Mr. David Kamau
Marketing & Communications
Website content, social media, PR & outreach

Mr. Kamau manages CBI's public communications, social media presence, and outreach to prospective students across East Africa.
