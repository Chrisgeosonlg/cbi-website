Your Impact

Why Give to CBI?

Calvary Bible Institute runs on the generosity of churches, individuals, and ministry partners. Your gift helps keep theological education affordable and accessible — especially for ministers in rural Tanzania who cannot afford full fees.

Your Gift Makes a Difference

  • ✦TZS 50,000 — sponsors study materials for one student per term
  • ✦TZS 150,000 — funds a full online course for a rural pastor
  • ✦TZS 500,000 — provides a full semester scholarship
  • ✦TZS 1,000,000+ — names you as a CBI Institutional Partner
